PLAYFULL

Playfull is an app where you can play games anytime, anywhere and get real rewards. They've partnered with Subway, Chick-fil-A, Denny's, Coffee Bean and more. Their games include mini-games similar to Candy Crush and a geolocation-based scavenger hunt similar to Poekmon GO.

They have also attended Startup UCLA's summer accelerator and have made it into Techstar LA's class of fall 2017. Learn more abou tthem at beplayfull.com

Mechanodontics

At Mechanodontics we are making a new type of braces for orthodontic treatment. Everything we do is focused on helping you achieve your perfect smile in a shorter time, with less pain, and also without anyone knowing you are wearing braces! They recently won first place in the Lowell Milken Institute Sandler Prize for New Entrepreneurs, receiving $70,000 to create their product.

Brain

We created an Internet of Things (IoT) marketplace, where users can discover and purchase IoT products. The website features a simple, yet aesthetically focused user interface that will allow the user to navigate intuitively. From there, the user can read reviews about the device, answer questions pertaining to compatibility with their existing interface, and above all, purchase IoT devices.

Voucial

Voucial is an app that leverages social media to offer a scalable solution to the most powerful and under-utilised marketing channel in the business world: referral marketing. It allows users to share photos of their purchased goods and services on their social media to redeem voucher and coupon incentives given by businesses. This way businesses are able to gain a great exposure to an exponential number of likeminded target audience and through the power of referral marketing.
