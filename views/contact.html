Interested in pursuing your entrepreneurial spirit? We are looking for motivated individuals passionate about pursuing their business ideas to Join Startup Labs. Startup Labs is a student run accelerator whose mission is to educate, assist, and consult students in the early stages of pursuing their business idea. Little experience is needed, students will get a chance to develop their idea throughout the program and learn the fundamental skills necessary for ideas to succeed.

Applications open at the start of the Fall quarter! Check out the application page!

Intern with us

Startup Labs is looking for interns to help us run the program! If you think you’ve got the experience to start helping startups, or if you’re just really ready to learn how, shoot us an email at startuplabs@bruinentrepreneurs.org with your resume.

Join our network of entrepreneurs

We’re always looking for new mentors, sponsors, and partners. If you're interested in helping the entrepreneurship community at UCLA or if you have any questions, email us at startuplabs@bruinentrepreneurs.org!
